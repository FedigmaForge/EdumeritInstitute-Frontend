Privacy Policy

edumerits.co.in

Last Updated: 15 March 2026

Welcome to EduMerits (edumerits.co.in). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and phone number
  • Email address
  • Educational details
  • Any information provided through forms or enquiries

Non-Personal Information

  • Browser type and device information
  • IP address
  • Pages visited on the website
  • Time spent on the website

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide educational services
  • To respond to enquiries
  • To contact you regarding courses or updates
  • To improve our website and services
  • To maintain internal records and analytics

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law
  • With trusted service providers who help operate the website
  • To protect the rights and safety of our organization

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience and understand website traffic and user behaviour. You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of internet transmission is completely secure.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their individual privacy policies.

7. Children's Information

Our website is intended for students and professionals seeking educational guidance. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
